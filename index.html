10

Security & risk — stated plainly.

No protocol that handles trust should hide its limitations. Here is exactly where Omen stands today.

Audit status
Not yet audited. Targeting a third-party audit ahead of mainnet (Q1 2027).
Admin control today
Badge minting and trust score updates are currently gated behind a single AdminCap, held by the founder, during this testnet phase.
Sybil resistance
Epoch-based rate limiting is enforced at the contract level — a hard cap on total applications per epoch, and one application per address per epoch.
Key loss
Social recovery via guardian proposals (propose_recovery, approve_recovery) exists in the contract today — not yet battle-tested.
Dispute resolution
Slashing today requires an active AuditorBadge holder. No appeals process yet — explicit known gap on the roadmap.
